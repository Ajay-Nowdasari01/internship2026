📚 What You Learned:

  • ✅ Conductors allow electricity to flow (metals like copper, iron)
  • ✅ Insulators block electricity (plastic, rubber, wood)
  • ✅ Conductors are used inside wires and cables
  • ✅ Insulators are used outside wires to keep us safe
